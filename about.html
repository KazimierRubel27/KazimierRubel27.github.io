All about Curious George

    One of the most adored and recognizable cartoon characters ever is Curious George. This cheeky little monkey was created by Hans and Margret Rey, a husband and wife team, and it has won over people's hearts for decades. Because of his boundless curiosity and spirit of adventure, Curious George has come to represent wonder and exploration.

    Curious George is a mischievous monkey, but he always means well. Despite the mayhem and confusion that his adventures may cause, he has a good heart, which makes him a lovable figure for kids everywhere.

    September 17th is recognized as Curious George Day in the United States. On this day, children are encouraged to embrace their own curiosity and pay tribute to the publication of the first Curious George book.
